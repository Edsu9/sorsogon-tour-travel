Discover the wonders of Sorsogon

Explore Sorsogon

A province of natural wonders and cultural treasures

Sorsogon, the southernmost province of Luzon, is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. From pristine beaches and islands to majestic volcanoes and wildlife encounters, Sorsogon offers diverse experiences for every type of traveler.

Our carefully curated selection of destinations showcases the best that Sorsogon has to offer. Each location has been chosen for its natural beauty, cultural significance, or unique experiences that you won't find anywhere else.

Donsol

Home of the gentle giants

About Donsol

Once a sleepy fishing village, Donsol has transformed into a world-renowned ecotourism destination famous for its whale shark (locally known as "butanding") interactions. What sets Donsol apart is its commitment to responsible wildlife tourism, where these gentle giants are observed in their natural habitat without feeding or disturbing them.

Beyond whale sharks, Donsol offers beautiful beaches, firefly watching along the Ogod River, and a glimpse into authentic Filipino coastal life.

Top Experiences

  • Swim with whale sharks (seasonal: November to June)
  • Firefly watching tour on the Ogod River
  • Relax on Donsol's beaches
  • Visit the Butanding Interaction Center

Getting There

Donsol is approximately 1.5 hours by road from Sorsogon City or 1 hour from Legazpi City. We provide comfortable transportation to and from Donsol as part of our tour packages.

Matnog

Gateway to pink beaches and hidden coves

About Matnog

Located at the southernmost tip of Luzon, Matnog is home to some of the most stunning beaches and islands in the Philippines. The area is famous for its pink beaches, crystal-clear waters, and marine sanctuaries teeming with life.

Matnog offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure, with opportunities for island hopping, snorkeling, and swimming in pristine waters surrounded by lush landscapes.

Top Experiences

  • Visit the pink sand beaches of Subic Beach
  • Island hopping to Tikling Island and Calintaan Island
  • Snorkeling at Juag Lagoon Marine Sanctuary
  • Explore the Calintaan Cave

Getting There

Matnog is approximately 2 hours by road from Sorsogon City. Our tour packages include comfortable transportation to and from Matnog, as well as boat arrangements for island hopping.

Bulusan Volcano

The Switzerland of the Orient

About Bulusan

Bulusan Volcano Natural Park is a protected area that showcases the majestic Bulusan Volcano and its surrounding forests, lakes, and hot springs. Often called the "Switzerland of the Orient" for its cool climate and lush landscapes, this area offers a refreshing escape from the tropical heat.

The centerpiece is Bulusan Lake, a serene crater lake surrounded by dense rainforest, creating a perfect setting for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Top Experiences

  • Trek through the Bulusan Volcano Natural Park
  • Kayaking on Bulusan Lake
  • Relax in Masacrot Hot Springs
  • Birdwatching in the forest sanctuary

Getting There

Bulusan is approximately 1 hour by road from Sorsogon City. Our tours include transportation and guided treks with experienced local guides who know the area intimately.

Pilar

Historical landmarks and natural wonders

About Pilar

Pilar offers a blend of historical significance and natural beauty. The municipality is home to important historical landmarks, beautiful beaches, and the stunning Panumbagan Island with its white sand and clear waters.

Visitors to Pilar can experience authentic local culture, sample fresh seafood, and explore both historical sites and natural attractions in one area.

Top Experiences

  • Visit the historic Pilar Church
  • Day trip to Panumbagan Island
  • Sample fresh seafood at the local market
  • Swimming and beach activities

Getting There

Pilar is approximately 30 minutes by road from Sorsogon City. Our tours include comfortable transportation and local guides to help you discover the best of Pilar.

Sorsogon City

The heart of the province

About Sorsogon City

As the capital of Sorsogon province, Sorsogon City serves as the commercial and cultural hub of the region. The city offers a glimpse into local life, with its bustling markets, historical buildings, and vibrant food scene.

Sorsogon City is also the perfect base for exploring the rest of the province, with easy access to all major attractions while providing modern amenities and accommodations.

Top Experiences

  • Visit the Sorsogon Cathedral
  • Stroll along the Rompeolas Bay Walk
  • Shop for local products at the public market
  • Sample local cuisine at city restaurants

Getting There

Sorsogon City is accessible by bus from Manila (12-14 hours) or by plane to Legazpi City followed by a 1-hour drive. We offer airport pickups from Legazpi and can arrange transportation from Manila upon request.
