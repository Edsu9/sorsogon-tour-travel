Directions

From Legazpi Airport: Take the national highway towards Sorsogon City (approximately 1 hour). Once in Sorsogon City, turn right at the main intersection near the cathedral and continue for 500 meters. Our office is on the left side, across from the public market.

From Sorsogon Bus Terminal: Take a tricycle and ask to be dropped at Explore Maps Travel and Tours near the city center (approximately 10 minutes).

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What is the best time to visit Sorsogon?

The best time to visit Sorsogon is during the dry season from November to May. For whale shark encounters in Donsol, the peak season is from February to April. However, Sorsogon can be visited year-round, with each season offering different experiences.

How do I book a tour with Explore Maps?

You can book a tour by filling out our contact form, sending us an email, or calling our office. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance, especially during peak season, to ensure availability.

Do you offer customized tour packages?

Yes, we specialize in creating customized itineraries based on your interests, schedule, and budget. Just let us know your preferences, and our team will design a personalized Sorsogon experience for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, credit cards, and cash payments. For tour bookings, we typically require a 50% deposit to secure your reservation, with the balance due upon arrival.

What should I pack for a trip to Sorsogon?

We recommend packing light, breathable clothing, swimwear, sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen), comfortable walking shoes, and a light rain jacket or umbrella. For whale shark encounters, bring your own rash guard if possible, though rentals are available.
