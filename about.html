Our Story

How Explore Maps began

Founded in 2015, Explore Maps Travel and Tours was born from a passion for showcasing the hidden gems of Sorsogon to the world. What started as a small operation with just two tour guides has grown into a premier travel service provider in the region.

Our journey began when our founder, a native of Sorsogon, realized that many travelers were missing out on the authentic experiences that this beautiful province has to offer. With deep local knowledge and a commitment to sustainable tourism, we set out to create tours that would not only delight visitors but also benefit local communities.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of travelers create unforgettable memories in Sorsogon, while maintaining our commitment to responsible tourism practices and supporting local economies.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional travel experiences that showcase the natural beauty and cultural richness of Sorsogon, while promoting sustainable tourism practices that preserve the environment and benefit local communities.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier travel service provider in Sorsogon, known for creating authentic, memorable experiences that connect travelers with the heart and soul of our province, while setting the standard for responsible tourism in the region.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide us

Sustainability
